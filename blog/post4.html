Ram Murmu Biography

Early Life and Education

Ram Murmu was born on November 5, 1993, in Mayurbhanj, Odisha, India. He completed his primary education at a local school in his village and later moved to Bhubaneswar for higher studies. Murmu pursued a degree in engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Rourkela.

Career

After completing his graduation, Murmu started working as a software engineer at a leading IT company in Bengaluru. He worked on several projects, including the development of a mobile app for a popular e-commerce platform. In 2020, Murmu decided to pursue his passion for writing and started working as a full-time author.

Works

  • The Story of a Village Boy (2020) - a novel that explores the life of a young boy growing up in a rural village in India.
  • The Journey of a Thousand Miles (2022) - a collection of short stories that delve into the lives of people from different walks of life.

Awards and Recognition

Murmu's work has been widely acclaimed and has received several awards and recognition. His novel, The Story of a Village Boy, was shortlisted for the prestigious Sahitya Akademi Award in 2021.

Personal Life

Murmu is married to Smt. Rukmini Murmu and they have two children together. He is a nature lover and enjoys spending time in the outdoors. Murmu is also a passionate advocate for social justice and has worked with several non-profit organizations to promote education and healthcare in rural areas.
