The Story of Ram Murmu

Introduction

Ram Murmu, the 15th President of India, is a man of humble beginnings and extraordinary achievements. Born on November 5, 1993, in Mayurbhanj, Odisha, Murmu's life is a testament to the power of hard work, determination, and perseverance. In this story, we will explore the journey of Ram Murmu, from his childhood to his presidency, and the lessons we can learn from his remarkable life.

Childhood and Education

Murmu was born to a poor tribal family in the remote village of Uparbeda, Mayurbhanj. His parents, Shri Rameshwar Tudu and Smt. Sulata Tudu, were farmers who worked hard to provide for their family. Despite the financial struggles, Murmu's parents encouraged his education and supported his passion for learning. He completed his primary education at a local school in his village and later moved to Bhubaneswar for higher studies.

Early Career and Politics

After completing his graduation, Murmu started his career as a junior engineer in the Government of Odisha. He worked hard and quickly rose through the ranks, becoming a respected leader in his community. In 2000, Murmu joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and began his journey in politics. He worked tirelessly, serving in various roles, including as a councilor, MLA, and Minister in the Government of Odisha.

Presidency

In 2022, Murmu was elected as the 15th President of India, becoming the youngest person to hold the office. As President, Murmu has worked to promote education, healthcare, and economic development in India. He has also been a strong advocate for the rights of tribal communities and has worked to empower women and marginalized groups.

Lessons from Ram Murmu's Life

Ram Murmu's life is a shining example of what can be achieved through hard work, determination, and perseverance. His story teaches us the importance of:

  • Education: Murmu's education was the key to his success, and he has always emphasized the importance of education in his life and work.
  • Hard work: Murmu's rise to the presidency was not easy, and he had to work hard to achieve his goals.
  • Perseverance: Despite facing many challenges and setbacks, Murmu never gave up and continued to work towards his goals.
